Higher or Lower

This Card game was my first ever project using basic HTML, CSS, and JS, Try your luck to beat the dealer but the dealer doesn't play fair! Saps your points anytime you guess wrong, you start at 30 points and the dealer starts at 50. If you click the ace button and guess the ace wrong you lose double the points. The game is deployed so enjoy it if you want to!
